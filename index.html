Process

Iterative, agile delivery with clear checkpoints and continuous feedback.

1

Discovery

Collaborate with stakeholders to define goals, constraints, and success metrics. Build an initial product backlog with prioritized user stories and acceptance criteria. Establish the definition of "done" and identify your product owner.

2

Plan

Design architecture and select optimal technologies based on your requirements, team capabilities, and long-term maintainability. Create examples and mockups for UI-facing work. Break deliverables into sprint-sized increments, and define timeline estimates and communication channels.

3

Build

Develop in short, focused iterations with continuous integration and frequent demos. Each sprint delivers working functionality, allowing for feedback and course correction. Secure defaults, code reviews, and visible progress throughout.

4

Handoff

Conduct final sprint review and retrospective. Deliver comprehensive documentation, runbooks, and knowledge transfer sessions. Provide a prioritized backlog of future enhancements and a clean transition plan that preserves your team's autonomy.

Products & Brands

These platforms are designed, built, and operated by Dispatch Dataworks LLC, combining modern technology with a strong focus on reliability, privacy, and community impact.

CNY911

Real-time community incident and emergency information

CNY911 is a high-traffic community information platform delivering timely incident alerts, weather updates, and situational awareness tools for Central New York. The site integrates machine learning and AI-assisted processing, geospatial mapping, and real-time data pipelines to organize and surface relevant information at scale.

Built to support heavy daily usage, CNY911 emphasizes performance, reliability, and search visibility. The platform averages nearly 10,000 unique visitors per day, with strong organic reach driven by search optimization and deep social media integration, including an engaged community of 11,000+ Facebook followers. Robust content moderation and spam controls help maintain trust, accuracy, and signal quality as the platform grows.

Visit: cny911.com

Nurserly

Privacy-first baby and family tracking

Nurserly is a secure family tracking application created by working parents to simplify collaboration around infant care. The platform supports shared tracking of feedings, sleep, diapers, medications, and milestones while keeping data private, accessible, and easy to understand.

Nurserly is built on more than 15 years of experience in healthcare IT, with design decisions informed by HIPAA-aligned practices for privacy, security, and data handling. While not a medical billing platform, Nurserly is engineered with compliance-grade safeguards to protect sensitive family data and give parents confidence in how their information is stored and shared.

Visit: nurserly.app

CNYMesh

Community-driven network resilience

CNYMesh is a community initiative focused on expanding decentralized, resilient mesh networking across Central New York. The project promotes experimentation, education, and infrastructure independence to support local communications and emergency readiness.
